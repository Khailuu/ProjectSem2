Returns & Refund Policy

Returns

Before returning an item, please contact Cozy Customer Care at (888)123-456-789 to obtain detailed shipping instructions. In general, if you are dissatisfied with your purchase, 2Modern will be happy to process a return for you, contingent upon the following:

Items must be unused, unassembled, in their original condition, and original packaging, complete with all tags, instructions, and inserts. All returns must be post-marked within 30 days of receiving the item. Unless otherwise specified by Cozy, all returns must be made via the original shipping method through which the order arrived (UPS, Freight, White Glove, etc.). Detailed shipping instructions will be provided by Cozy. Please allow at least 2 weeks after your order has been received for your refund to be processed at which point you will be refunded the full purchase price, including tax, minus shipping. For items offered with free shipping, outbound shipping fees will be deducted from your final refund.

Exceptions - We cannot accept returns of:

  • Made-to-Order Products

  • Products identified as non-returnable

  • Products which have been installed or assembled

  • Products not in their original condition and original packaging

  • Products which have been successfully delivered and signed for via White Glove Delivery

  • Open Box Items

  • Gift Cards

Damaged & Defective Items

If you have received an item that was incorrect, defective, or has been damaged during shipment, please contact Cozy Customer Care at (888) 123-456-789immediately. Please do not send back these items prior to contacting us. Do not try to repair any product unless directed in writing by 2Modern or the manufacturer.

When receiving your item, please be sure to inspect for damage before signing for the package. If damage is visible, reject the shipment, be sure the driver notes the damage and contact Cozy Customer Care at (888) 123-456-789. For damages concealed at the time of delivery, promptly take photos of the damaged item and the packaging it arrived in, and contact Cozy Customer Care at (888) 123-456-789.

Please note, if you have received a damaged product that can be remedied with replacement parts, and choose to return an item rather than accept replacement parts, you will be refunded the amount of your order less any unpaid shipping charges (see above).

Cozy is not responsible for any delays in your project or labor costs due to defective or damaged products or delays in receiving product.
