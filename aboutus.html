Our Distinguished Design Process

INDUSTRY LEADING PROCESS FOR A SEAMLESS PROJECT

Over the course of our years in business, we have developed an efficient 12-step process that is the cornerstone of our work. We value our clients' time and investment and prioritize connection and transparency throughout each project. Each phase is an intentional step toward your home's highest potential.

Project phase one: Design

Every project begins with a one-on-one consultation. Once our initial proposal is approved, contracts are signed, retainer is paid, and client homework is complete, we are able to turn our focus to visualizing and curating each element. We will schedule a Discovery Meeting, wherein we will take on-site measurements and photos. Next, we will begin sourcing visual inspiration and piecing together concepts. As our initial plans take form, we will compile material samples, drawings, and furnishings in a visual and tactile presentation that will help you get a real sense of how your new home will feel and function.

Project phase two: Ordering

When the design of your home is finalized, we move on to sourcing the materials, textiles, design elements, fixtures and furnishings we've selected. This phase goes beyond ordering as our Procurement Manager, Lisa, tracks lead times, coordinates deliveries with our receiving warehouse, ensures each item is inspected, and schedules assembly of any furnishings ahead of installation. At this point in the process, we take a step back to review your budget and offer you updates so that you know what to expect in terms of total investment.

Project phase three: Installation

This is the phase when your home finally comes to life! Once construction is complete, we walk through your newâ€”or newly renovatedâ€”home together and take note of any tasks that remain. Next, we schedule an installation day for our team to deliver your furnishings, place them, and style them perfectly. Once we are finished, we welcome you home and have a final walkthrough to get your feedback. After we remedy any outstanding issues, we close out the project with a final budget meeting. Afterward, we celebrate a successful project and send you home to enjoy your new haven with a care and maintenance guide.
