  • Shipping & Delivery

    How long does it take for my products to be made and delivered?

    Production and delivery time varies depending on the item(s) ordered, your customization options, and your location. We aim to deliver within 4 weeks from the order date.

    How much is shipping?

    Shipping prices within EU countries are included in all FIT products. For shipping outside the EU, please contact us at Cozy-Fnt@gmail.com

    Can I pick up my order direct from FIT?

    Sorry, this is not possible. Currently we do not have a showroom or a store to pick-up products directly.

    How can I track my order?

    To track your order, you will receive an email with our delivery service tracking details of your order.

    How will I know how my order is progressing?

    Once your payment is received, your purchase is sent directly to the workshop. So that you can monitor the progress of your purchase you will receive three emails regarding the status of your order. You will be alerted by email at the following stages; Once your payment has been accepted Once your order is dispatched from our manufacturer into your chosen address Once your order has been delivered

  • Returns

    How do I return my purchase?

    FIT is dedicated to providing good customer support. If you wish to return an item(s), please send an email to hello@fit-furniture.com. We kindly ask you include your purchase order number so we can find your order details in the most convenient way.

    What happens if I don’t like the product once it arrives?

    We’re confident you will like your product But just in case, we have a Return for Any Reason policy. If you are unhappy, simply contact us to arrange for your return.

    What does “Return for Any Reason” mean?

    If you decide after receiving delivery of your product(s) you wish to return the product(s) for any reason, you can.

    What are the shipping charges in case of returns?

    You can return your item(s) within 14 days of the purchase date. If the product(s) is not damaged or faulty, and you simply decided that you do not want it anymore, you will receive a refund, less the outbound associated shipping costs. Shipping costs will vary according to the item(s) and your location. In the unexpected event of receiving a faulty product(s) or you have a warranty claim, please contact us by email at hello@fit-furniture.com. Photos may be requested to support the assessment of your claim. If the warranty claim is accepted, we will arrange a replacement, a store credit or a refund for the item(s), and cover all associated freight costs.

  • Warranty

    What are your Warranty Terms and Conditions?

    FIT warrants all goods for a period of three years under normal usage, to be free from defects in material and workmanship. FIT’s liability under this warranty is to repair or replace (at its discretion) any part or product covered by this (or any implied) warranty free of charge. This warranty does not cover: Normal wear and tear; Damage arising from abnormal use, or abuse; Products which have not been maintained, or which have been modified; Any indirect or consequential loss. This warranty applies only to the original purchaser. Order Number or invoice is considered proof of purchase. All works carried out by a party under instructions from FIT will be covered by the 12 month warranty on workmanship and repair. If the workmanship of the repair is deemed by FIT to be faulty a new item will be provided. All other warranties, express or implied, are excluded.

    How long is your warranty?

    All FIT’s products are backed by a three year warranty.

    What do I do if I have a problem with a FIT product?

    We will help you with any queries after your purchase. Contact us at hello@fit-furniture.com if you require any assistance with your product after purchase.

  • Pricing

    Are your prices in Euro (€)?

    All prices are in Euro (€).

    Do your prices include VAT?

    All prices include your country specific VAT.

    What payment methods do you offer?

    FIT accepts major credit cards, Paypal and Apple Pay.

  • Assembly

    How do I assemble my FIT product(s)?

    Each FIT product family is assembled differently. You will receive a set of intuitive and easy to follow instructions that guide you through the assembly process for your specific furniture piece. It’s advisable that you have someone to assist during assembly.

    What will I need to assemble my FIT product?

    FIT furniture need only be assembled by hand. But securing shelves to the wall will require a drill and screwdriver. For safety reasons, securing tall shelf units to the wall is advisable.

    How long will it take to assemble my FIT product?

    This depends on the product you have purchased, however we have planned that assembly is a quick and simple process.

    Can I dissemble my FIT furniture for transportation?

    Absolutely, FIT furniture can be easily disassembled for easy transportation. We do recommend you take care when disassembling your furniture though, as rough handling may lead to chipping or denting of your FIT product.
