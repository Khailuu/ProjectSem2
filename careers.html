We’re more than just a business...We’re a family

As a privately held, family-owned company spanning three generations, we realize success isn’t achieved in silos. It comes from the collective talents of each of our valued teammates. That’s why when the Wanek family founded Ashely in 1945, it was built upon a strong foundation that included hard work, an unwavering commitment to excellence and a passion for innovation. Our humble roots, steeped in service to our clients, customers and more than 35,000 teammates across the globe have helped us become the largest manufacturer of furniture in the world and largest retail furniture store brand in North America.

We know none of the success we’ve achieved would be possible without the dedication and tenacity of all our valued team members, who we consider to be an extension of our own family. As we work together to continuously improve our collective goal of being the best furniture company in the world, we invite you to become part of the family so we can win together.
