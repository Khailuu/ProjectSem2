PRODUCT INTRODUCTION

Vertigo Pendant Light is a unique and impressive pendant light product from the brand Petite Friture. Designed by Constance Guisset, Vertigo Pendant Light has a light and harmonious shape, like a wide-brimmed hat flying in the air. This pendant light is made of ultra-light fiberglass, covered by soft polyurethane fibers, creating a sturdy and flexible structure. When hanging from the ceiling, Vertigo Pendant Light creates unique shadows on the wall, creating a cozy and romantic space. Vertigo Pendant Light comes in various colors and sizes to suit different interior styles. Vertigo Pendant Light is a high-class and luxurious pendant light product that will highlight your living space.
